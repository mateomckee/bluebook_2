NEWBOOK

Welcome to UTSA Newbook!

Newbook is UTSA's newest course catalog: a tool that allows you to easily search and evaluate over 1000 different courses, syllabi, and professors!

  • Quick search across thousands of courses
  • Access every course's syllabus
  • Professor and course ratings by students

Newbook was made by a group of UTSA students as part of ACM, Rowdy Creators. Their goal is to make course registration and degree planning easier at UTSA.
